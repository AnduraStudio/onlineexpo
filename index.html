Platform

This will be our access platform, here you will be able to read more about us and our product, look at past events and what will happen besides making your access registration.

Here you can have a preview of the events that we did and that we will do, it will be available for you to access photos and videos of these events together with a brief explanation, this option will be available to everyone who access the site.

Accreditation on the platform will be carried out here, a simple and quick process, which can also be carried out through an automatic connection to social networks or google account and within it the person will answer questions about their interests at the event.

Exhibition space and stands

You can freely walk around the fair and explore everything at your own pace.

Interactions will be possible with some tablets or screens or NPC that will direct you to the landing page of the company you were interested in.

By signing the platform you will have access to the stands for your fair, and you can also bring your models or hire our production service to adapt the stands.

Auditorium

The auditorium is a very important part of the event. It will be possible to transmit recorded videos or lives.

The speaker will be able to interact through a chat with his audience, and will also have a moderation system for him.

If it is in the interest of the speaker and any person at the lecture, both may enter a VIP room, within which a web conference will be available.

Live room

This space will be used for shows. Live or recorded presentation is possible.

The audience's interaction with the artist will be possible through chat and reactions and will also have a moderation system for the artist.

Network/Business Match

We believe that it is extremely important to value the social part of the event, thus making it easier when making business and connections, which are one of the most important, if not the most.

A search panel to find guests quickly will be available with profile analysis, if the user identifies with the other, he can give a business match and it is possible to schedule a meeting by web conference or just by chat.
